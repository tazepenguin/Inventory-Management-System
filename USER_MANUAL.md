# User Manual

## Logging In
Access the system at your domain. Use your username and password.

## Dashboard
- View total products, low stock alerts, recent transactions, and stock by location chart.

## Managing Products
- Add, edit, delete products. Each product has SKU, name, category, price, reorder level.
- Use the search and filter to find products.

## Recording Transactions
- Use "Add Transaction" to record stock in, out, or transfer between locations.
- Stock levels update automatically.

## Sales Orders
- Create customers, then create a sales order.
- Add items to the order, confirm it, then fulfill from a location (stock is reduced).

## Reports
- Export product list as CSV.
- View stock by location chart.

## Barcode Scanning
- Click "Scan Barcode" on the product list page, point camera at barcode to search.
